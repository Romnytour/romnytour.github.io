About Us

Romny Tour Express Ferry is the fastest and most reliable way to travel between the mainland and the Romny Tour Express Ferry is a reliable and convenient transport service between Sihanoukville, Koh Rong, and Koh Rong Sanloem. Their modern ferries are equipped with comfortable seating, air conditioning, and onboard amenities, ensuring a pleasant and enjoyable journey. In addition to their regular ferry services, Romny Tour Express Ferry also offers a variety of tour packages, including snorkeling tours, fishing tours, and island hopping tours. This makes them a one-stop shop for all your travel needs between Sihanoukville, Koh Rong, and Koh Rong Samloem.
